Madison is home to some of my favorite restaraunts. They have every cusine you could possibly want ranging from Italian to American to Asian. It is also so fun to try out new places with my friends. These are photos of some of my favorite dishes I have had this year.

This is my favorite salad from Ian's Pizza. You can customize it and make it your own.

Monty's Blue Plate Diner is my favorite place to get brunch in Madison. The diner vibe is super special and the food never fails to impress.

Bar Corallini has the best Italian food in Madison. The 8 Finger Cavatelli is my favorite pasta dish.

Eno Vinois a classic spot in Madison. The views from the restaraunt are unmatched and the food never misses.

The Globe is the most underrated spot in Madison. The Korean barbaque and mango lassi are my go to's.
